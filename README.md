### About

I build local-first AI systems with practical controls, durable storage, and interfaces that make automation easier to trust.

Right now I am focused on personal AI assistants, WhatsApp automation, Postgres-backed memory, local LLM workflows, and dashboards that expose the important operational state without noise.

### Engineering Taste

- Local-first when privacy and reliability matter.
- Clear dashboards over hidden automation.
- Durable state before clever behavior.
- Small safety checks around anything that sends files, messages, or actions.
- Shipping useful tools first, then making them cleaner.
