### About

I am a B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Sri Sai Ram Engineering College, Chennai, building across AI, IoT, computer vision, embedded systems, web apps, and cloud-backed products.

My work is centered on practical AI systems: healthcare access, elderly care, human activity recognition, scam detection, local automation, and interfaces that make technical workflows easier to use.
